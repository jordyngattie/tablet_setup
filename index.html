Samsung Tab A (2017) Setup Guide

Setup Instructions

  1. Open the package (or perform a factory reset if the device is not new).
  2. Turn on the device. Select English (Canada) as the language.
  3. Review Information:
    Select End User License Agreement and Privacy Policy, then click Next.
  4. Network Connection: Select Skip, then click Next.
  5. Connect to Wi-Fi: Enter password [INSERT INFO], select Don’t Validate, click Next.
  6. Google Sign-In: Select Skip. Do NOT log into a Google account.
  7. Google Services: Turn OFF all three Google services and click Accept.
  8. Set Device Security:
    Select Pattern, enable Require pattern when device powers on, and create the following pattern:
    Example of the required “G” pattern
  9. Click Continue, repeat the pattern, and click Confirm.
    Skip Samsung account/services at the bottom, confirm Skip again.
    Click Finish to arrive at the home screen.
    If asked about Google checking your device for security, Decline.
    If prompted to turn on location services, Decline.
    Swipe down to open the settings menu (gear wheel).
    Go to Settings → Display → Screen timeout and change from 30 seconds to 5 minutes.

    10. Samsung Tab A 2017 – ODK Collect Installation


    1. From the home screen, navigate to Settings > Security > Install unknown apps > Samsung Internet, then Allow
    2. From the home screen, open the web browser (Internet). Cancel any “Update Samsung Internet”. Visit [insert url]tinyurl.com/mcgillsetup in the address bar.
    3. Click to download ODK Collect APK: Download ODK Collect
    4. When prompted by Samsung Internet, click Allow.
    5. Under Choose an Action, select Downloads.
    6. Open the downloaded file (swipe down and click the notification, or find it in Downloads via My Files).
    7. Click Install.

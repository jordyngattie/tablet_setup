Setup Instructions

  1. Open the package.
    If the device is not new, perform a factory reset first.
  2. Turn on the device.
    Select English (Canada) as the language.
  3. Review Information.
    Select:
    • End User License Agreement
    • Privacy Policy
    Tap Next (bottom right).
  4. Network Connection.
    When asked “How do you want to connect?” select Skip, then tap Next.
  5. Connect to Wi-Fi.
    Select the correct Wi-Fi network.
    Enter password: [INSERT INFO]
    If prompted, select Don’t Validate, then tap Next.
  6. Google Sign-In.
    Select Skip.
    Do NOT log into a Google account.
  7. Google Services.
    Turn OFF all three Google services and tap Accept.
  8. Set Device Security.
    Select Pattern.
    Enable Require pattern when device powers on.
    Create the following pattern when prompted:
  • Continue Setup.
    1. Click Continue, repeat the pattern, and click Confirm.
    2. At the bottom of the screen, choose to Skip Samsung account / services. Confirm Skip again.
    3. Click Finish. You should arrive at the home screen.
    4. If you are ever asked whether Google may regularly check your device for security problems, Decline.
    5. You may also be prompted to turn on location services, Decline.
    6. Swipe the screen down to find the settings menu (gear wheel). Use that for the following:
    7. Settings → Display → Screen timeout: Change it from 30 seconds to 5 minutes.
